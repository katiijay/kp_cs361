Help and Instructions

How to use our site

General Use

This site is intended to be a repository of all of your upcoming camping trips. You can view, create, update or remove trips all on this application.

View Trips

  • Clicking on any of the Trip Names will allow you to see the details of a camping trip.
  • Adding a new trip will allow you to add a new trip to your list (see Add/Update a trip below)
  • Updating a trip will let you change the details of a trip you've already created (see Add/Update a trip below)
  • Deleting a trip will remove it from your list. Please note, this is a permanent action! If you want to instead change the trip, please use the "Update" option instead.

Add/Update a Trip

Adding and updating a trip are extremely similar. The only difference is that "adding" will create a new trip on your list, and "updating" will alter an existing trip that you've already created. The information in the form is as follows.:

  • Trip Name: This is a required field that should contain a custom name you can give the trip so you can quickly identify it. While it's not required, it is helpful to make this name unique so you can distinguish it from other trips.
  • Trip Start Date: This is a required field. It is the date you wish to start your trip.
  • Trip End Date: This is the date you wish to end your trip.
  • Campground Name: This is a required field. This drop-down field allows you to select from a list of known National Parks Campgrounds.
  • Submit Button: This button confirms your changes and will direct you to view the trip once complete. If you made a mistake, you can choose to "Update" from that page!

About the National Parks

There are officially 63 National Parks in the United States, and an additional 370 designated Parks in the Parks System. To find out more, visit the US National Parks website.
